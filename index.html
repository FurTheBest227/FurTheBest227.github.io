About Pre-Algia

The vision for Pre-Algia is to make it similar to pawn shops, but as a website where you can buy, sell, or trade rare items. You can communicate 1-on-1 online and make the best purchase with other people and the staff at Pre-Algia. We always strive to ensure fairness, and if you ever need guidance when making a trade or purchase, we can help assess if it's fair for both parties.
